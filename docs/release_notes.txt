## My Healthy Food - Initial Release

Welcome to the first release of My Healthy Food!

**Key Features in this version:**

* **AI-Powered Recipe Generation:** Generate unique and delicious recipes in any language using our advanced AI.
* **Multi-Language Support:** Create recipes in various languages to explore global cuisines.
* **Real-time Web Search Integration:** Get relevant and up-to-date recipes with pre-search functionality.
* **Rich Recipe Rendering:** Enjoy beautifully formatted and easy-to-read recipes.
* **Social Sharing:** Share your culinary creations with friends and family.
* **Image Integration:** Recipes are enhanced with relevant images from Unsplash and Google Images.
* **User Authentication:** Create secure accounts to save your favorite recipes and personalize your experience.
* **User Feedback System:**  Provide feedback directly within the app to help us improve.

**Known Issues:**

* Initial release, may contain minor bugs.
* Performance may vary depending on network conditions and device.

Thank you for trying My Healthy Food! We are excited to bring you more features and improvements in future updates.  Please send us your feedback!
