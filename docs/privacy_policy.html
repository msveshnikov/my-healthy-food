Privacy Policy for My Healthy Food

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use the My Healthy Food application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • User Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and password.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as recipes you generate, searches you perform, and features you use.
  • Feedback Data: If you provide feedback, we will collect your comments and suggestions.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the My Healthy Food app.
  • Personalize your experience.
  • Respond to your feedback and requests.
  • Send you updates and information about the app (with your consent, where required).

Sharing Your Information

We will not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect our rights and property.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
